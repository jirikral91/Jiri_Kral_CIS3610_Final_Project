What is ISL?

The International Swimming League (ISL) is an annual professional swimming league, established in 2019. It features a team-based competition format with fast-paced race sessions. The regular season starts in October and the Final Match is held in December.

Swimming is a low-impact sporting activity with a huge transformational potential for society. It can be safely used to promote public health and healthy lifestyles and as a result it can increase the rates of engagement.

We aim to create new groundbreaking projects, new in both form and content, which would explore the full potential of competitive swimming and secure sustainable commercial growth in the sport.

-ISL

Season 3 Grand Finale

The International Swimming League is pleased to announce that the ISL Season 3 FINAL will take place on 3-4 December 2021 at the Pieter van den Hoogenband Zwemstadion, in Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

Following the ISL Season 3 Play-offs, to be held from 11 to 28 November, the four best ISL teams will compete in the Season 3 Final match on Friday, 3 December and Saturday, 4 December, when the ISL Season 3 Champions will be crowned.

The competitions will be held at the legendary Pieter van den Hoogenband Zwemstadion, Antoon Coolenlaan 1, Eindhoven, from 19:00 to 21:00 CET on both day

ISL Season 3 Top Ranked Swimmers

Ilya Shymanovich

Ilya is a Belarusian swimmer. He is the world record holder in the short course 100 metre breaststroke and is one of the two current world record holders in the short course 50 metre breaststroke.

Caeleb Dressel

Caeleb is an American professional swimmer who specializes in freestyle, butterfly, and individual medley events. He swims representing the Cali Condors. Caeleb is a recent five time gold medalist at Tokyo Olympic games.

Elizabeth Nelson

Beata is a competitive swimmer who represents the United States and Cali Condors. In 2019, NCAA proclaimed her as female swimmer of the year, and sh is also currently top rank female swimmer in ISL.

Lilly King

Lilly is a competitive swimmer from USA representing Cali Condors in ISL. She is a silver and bronze medailist from Tokyo. In ISL, she has several wins in all breaststroke events and she is the current League record holder in all 3 breaststroke events.

Sarah Fredrica Sjostrom

Sarah is a competitive swimmer from Sweden representing Energy Standard. She is a silver medailist from Tokyo and one of the most talented swimemrs today. In ISL, she has everal wins in sprint events, such as 50 freestyle, 100 freestyle, 200 freestyle, 50 butterfly and 100 butterfly. She is also de current League record holder in the 50 freestyle.

Ryan Murphy

Ryan is a competitive swimmer from USA representing LA Current. He was part of the golden IM relay in Tokyo and also brought silver and bronz individual medals. In ISL, he has several wins in backstroke events and he is also the current league record holder in the 50 backstroke.
